About the Academy

Gurudevo Academy of Dance

Gurudevo Academy of Dance is dedicated to preserving and transmitting the classical art of Bharatanatyam in its purest form, while making it accessible within a contemporary, multicultural context.

Founded by Vidushi Megha Malar Prabhakar, the academy is rooted in the Kalakshetra tradition and emphasizes technical precision, artistic depth, and cultural understanding.

The training approach integrates discipline, structure, and expressive exploration-guiding students not only toward performance excellence, but toward a deeper connection with the art form as a medium of identity, storytelling, and inner growth.
